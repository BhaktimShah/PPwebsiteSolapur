Welcome to Picnic Point Solapur

Your Perfect Weekend Getaway in the Heart of Maharashtra

Discover Your Natural Escape

Located in the beautiful city of Solapur, Picnic Point is a sprawling 15-acre oasis of natural beauty and modern comfort. We've been creating unforgettable memories for families, friends, and corporate groups for over a decade.


Our meticulously maintained gardens, state-of-the-art facilities, and warm hospitality make us the premier choice for day outings, picnics, celebrations, and corporate events. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or an adventure-filled day, Picnic Point offers something special for everyone.


Just a short drive from Solapur city center, we provide the perfect blend of accessibility and natural serenity. Come experience why thousands of families choose us year after year.
